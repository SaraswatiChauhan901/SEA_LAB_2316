## 4. 1-Week Confirmed Case Growth (Top 10 Countries)

The line chart tracks changes in confirmed cases for the ten hardest-hit countries over the last week in your dataset. ## 5. Distribution of Case Fatality Rate (CFR)

The histogram shows how the case fatality rate (deaths per 100 confirmed cases) is distributed across all nations in your dataset. Most countries cluster at a lower CFR, likely under 5%, but there are always outlier nations with higher rates. These extremes may stem from overwhelmed health systems, populations at higher risk, limited access to care, or challenges in accurate case detection.

## Conclusion

The suite of visualizations uncovers major geographic, temporal, and outcome disparities in the impact of COVID-19 globally. Distinct country and regional patterns in cases, recoveries, deaths, and week-to-week growth illustrate that the pandemic does not affect all areas equally. These insights enable better decision-making for resource distribution, policy interventions, and international cooperation. Appropriate use of visual analytics—as demonstrated here—empowers health officials, researchers, and the public by making complex data understandable and actionable.
